MY PROJECTS

NWA Psych BEDS

This was a group project done under the McMillion Innovation Studio at the University of Arkansas. This project aimed to solve an issue with communication between hospitals in the Northwest Arkansas (NWA) area. As doctors diagnose their patients, they may come to the conclusion that their patient requires psychiatric care; however their facility may not offer that. This means they must find a different facility with available beds for their patient. Unfortunately, this is a tedious process that requires many phone calls and hours.

The goal of this project was to create a website that would allow the hospitals in the NWA area to easily find available psychiatric beds for their patients. Utilizing HTML, Python, and NoSQL, I mainly worked on developing the front end of the website while assisting with the back end development as needed. Eventually I was promoted to product manager, which meant in addition to developing, I was leading the team and communicating with potential consumers for feedback and making improvements as necessary.

SLS Community is a local organization that provides care for neurodivergent adults. At SLS Community, they utilize an ABC (Antecedent – Behavior – Consequence) form to provide behavior support to their community members. This form works by allowing staff to mark the different behaviors that the community member has displayed as well as what led up to the behavior and what happened afterwards. The objective of this project was to create an application that will allow SLS Community to better create, save, and review their ABC forms.

With the use of Flutter, Google Firebase, and Firestore we created a website that possesses the capability to collect ABC (Antecedent-Behavior-Consequence) data and store it in a secure and efficient manner. The users have the ability to view, add, and download ABC forms.
